Fake News Detector

'Fake news' is false or misleading information presented under the guise of factual information. It is often produced to damage a person's or entity's reputation or for the author's financial gain.Fake news, having the propensity of being more emotive and thus resulting in more clicks, often displaces real newsas a result of social media algorithms. The proliferation of fake news online is a serious concern as it has a significant negative impact on both individuals and society by spreading mistrust and confusion.

Machine learning algorithms utilising natural language processing (NLP) such as the one presented here, could begin to restore the balance between what is true and what is fake.
